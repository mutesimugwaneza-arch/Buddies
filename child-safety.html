Buddies App — Child Safety Standards

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Our Commitment

Buddies is committed to maintaining a safe platform for all users and has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

Age Requirement

Buddies is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly allow users under the age of 16 to create accounts or participate in experiences on our platform.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Buddies:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Grooming, exploitation, or solicitation of minors
  • Any content that endangers the safety of children

Detection and Prevention

Buddies takes the following steps to prevent CSAE on our platform:

  • All user-generated content is subject to our community guidelines
  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app
  • Reported content is reviewed promptly and actioned accordingly
  • Accounts found violating child safety policies are permanently banned
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns through the following channels:

  • In-app reporting: Tap the flag icon on any experience, profile, or message to report it
  • Email: smarthouses254@gmail.com with subject "Child Safety Report"
  • Emergency: If a child is in immediate danger, contact your local law enforcement immediately

Reporting to Authorities

Buddies complies with all relevant child safety laws and reports confirmed cases of CSAM to:

  • Regional and national law enforcement authorities
  • The National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable
  • Relevant local authorities in Rwanda, Kenya, and other operating regions
