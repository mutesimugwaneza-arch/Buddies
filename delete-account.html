Buddies App — Delete Account

To delete your account and all associated data:

Option 1 — In-app

  1. Open the Buddies app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Option 2 — Email request

Email us at support@buddies.app with subject "Delete My Account"

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, photo, bio)
  • Your friend connections
  • Your experience history
  • Your chat messages

What is retained

  • Transaction records for fraud prevention (90 days)
  • Anonymized activity logs (90 days)
