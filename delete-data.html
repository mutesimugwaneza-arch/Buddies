Buddies App — Delete My Data

You can request deletion of specific data without deleting your account.

Option 1 — In-app

  1. Open the Buddies app
  2. Go to Profile → Edit Profile
  3. Remove your photo, bio, or location manually

Option 2 — Email request

Email us at support@buddies.app with subject "Delete My Data"

Data you can request deleted

  • Profile photo
  • Bio and personal description
  • Location data
  • Chat history

Retention period

All requested data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.
